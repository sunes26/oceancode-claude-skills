## Caveat — survivor bias

The corpus contains only high-scoring posts. This data answers "when do top performers tend to post", not "when does posting maximize the chance of becoming a top performer". Treat the windows below as where the survivors cluster, not as a guarantee. With n=1945, samples are large enough that the windows are stable across refreshes.
